Warranty

At PC TECH, we stand behind the quality and reliability of our products, and your satisfaction is our priority. All purchases of our PCs come with a comprehensive warranty to provide you with peace of mind and assurance.

Our standard warranty covers your PC for a duration of 1 year from the date of purchase. This period ensures that you have ample time to thoroughly enjoy your computing experience without worrying about unexpected issues.
Our warranty encompasses a broad range of components and functionalities, including but not limited to: - Processor - Graphics Card - Motherboard - Memory (RAM) - Storage Drives - Power Supply Unit (PSU) - Cooling System - System Integration
During the warranty period, our dedicated support team is available to assist you with any technical queries or concerns. Whether you encounter hardware malfunctions, software issues, or need guidance on optimizing your system, our experts are here to help.
In the unlikely event of a covered issue, initiating a warranty claim is a straightforward process. Simply reach out to our customer support team via PCTECHteam@gmail.com, and we'll guide you through the necessary steps to resolve the issue promptly.
It's important to note that the warranty does not cover damages resulting from accidents, unauthorized modifications, or third-party components not supplied by PC TECH. Additionally, normal wear and tear, as well as issues arising from improper usage, are not covered under the warranty.
For added peace of mind, we offer extended warranty options to further protect your investment. Extend your coverage beyond the standard period to ensure continued support and protection for your PC.
